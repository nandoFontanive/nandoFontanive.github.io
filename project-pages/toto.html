Totó
Totó is a tabletop game designed to involve both children and adults in speech therapy.
Guided by professional speech therapists, ensuring precise phoneme articulation and a tailored learning experience:

  • A captivating fusion of technology and play, Totó's mini-games come to life through voice interaction.

  • As children accurately articulate phonemes, their minigame progress advances.

  • All thanks to the seamless integration of AI-driven recognition powered by the capabilities of Core ML.

With the Totó app, home speech therapy is now easier and more engaging than ever! Guided by professionals, you can effortlessly complete the best speech exercises through interactive minigames, challenges, and adorable dog companions.
