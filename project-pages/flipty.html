Flipty
Embark on an exciting journey with Flipty, the app that brings classic decision-making to your fingertips - conveniently accessible on your Apple Watch and iPhone! The thrill of tossing a coin or rolling a dice comes to life like never before. Just move your wrist up and down with your Apple Watch or shake your iPhone to experience the exciting animations and immersive sounds of these timeless games. Advanced accelerometer technology ensures seamless, realistic gameplay, making every movement feel authentic. Stay tuned for upcoming updates with additional minigames like Rock, Paper, Scissors, with multiplayer features for endless entertainment with friends and family. Plus, unlock different dice types and minigames for more variety and excitement.
Characteristics:

  • Heads or Tails: Experience the classic coin toss in stunning 3D.

  • Dice Roll: Shake your device to roll the dice and see what fate has in store.

  • Apple Watch Integration: Enjoy seamless gameplay on your wrist with Apple Watch support.

  • Realistic sound effects: Immerse yourself in the experience with authentic sounds in every playthrough.
