Senses
Senses is an application developed with the aim of encouraging users to reflect on their feelings. In it, users can select a deck of cards default or create your own. The app randomly chooses a card, challenging the player to perform some action.
In the pre-defined card decks, the user will find three types of games: Word Match, Guessing and Act Out.

  • Word Match: The user thinks of three synonyms for the feeling written on the card and the other players must try to guess the word.

  • Guessing: The other players must try to guess which personality was drawn just by asking yes or no questions.

  • Act Out: The player must try to represent the drawn emotion through mimes and the other players must try to guess.

With the possibility of creating new decks and cards, users can explore their creativity and create new ways to play! Ready to explore your emotions? Senses is available for download on the AppStore. Download now and have fun while connecting with yourself!
