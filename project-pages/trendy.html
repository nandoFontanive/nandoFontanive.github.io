Trendy
Trendy makes life worth living and is a memory keeper. Discover all the fun activities there are to make every moment count!

Discover fun and unusual activities with Trendy and capture each moment with photos and videos. Relive your memories anytime and share your adventures with friends!

Features:

  • Access to creative and exciting activity ideas.

  • Capture each experience with photos or videos.

  • Memory gallery to revisit special moments.

  • Activities categorized: Friends, Solo, Food, Arts, Explorer, Special, and Custom.

  • Explore new trends and challenges to live life to the fullest.

  • Enjoy social experiences that celebrate life and creativity.

Choose which category you want to find more activities:

Explorer: Engage in experiences that encourage you to explore the world around you.

Solo: Discover experiences you can do alone.

Friends: Find fun activities you can do with others.

Arts: Discover experiences that reveal your artistic talents (or not).
