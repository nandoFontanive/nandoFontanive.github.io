Acesso+
Acesso+ was designed to make your life easier by providing practical and objective information about accessibility and access routes to bars and restaurants on the PUCRS campus in Porto Alegre. The routes are calculated based on your location on the campus, always providing the shortest distance. Your search is even easier, with the use of filters and favorites features. Developed in 1 month, the app is already available on the App Store and uses the MapKit framework for its functionality.

AppStore: https://apple.co/4bUiVF5
