About me
Currently, I study Computer Science at PUCRS and work as an iOS developer at the Apple Developer Academy, with experience in SwiftUI and UIKit, as well as other Apple frameworks such as CoreMotion, CoreML, SpriteKit, and AudioKit. My interest in academic research in the area of data privacy led to the presentation of a paper at the 37th Annual IFIP WG 11.3 Conference on Data and Applications Security and Privacy (DBSec'23) in Sophia Antipolis, France.
As an iOS developer, my goal is to leverage my experience and knowledge in software development to create applications that deliver a high-level user experience. I am seeking challenging opportunities to collaborate on innovative projects, exploring new technologies while contributing to team and company growth. I am committed to continuously enhancing my technical and design skills, aiming to become a highly skilled professional in the iOS app development industry.
