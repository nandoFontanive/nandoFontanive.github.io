Cabide
Cabide was made especially for you, who are passionate about fashion and love planning your looks in advance! Developed as a team, this app simulates a virtual wardrobe, allowing you to photograph all your clothing items, create looks, organize collections and always be on top of trends! Don't waste time, Cabide is now available for download on the AppStore. Download right now and take your passion for fashion to the next level!
